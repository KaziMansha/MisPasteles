Learn More About Mis Pasteles:

Mis Pasteles, which was founded recently by our lovely founder Margarita, is a custom cake and cupcake business that uses all organic/natural ingredients. Margarita's cakes and cupcakes are perfect for any occasion as she caters to all needs! Not only does she use all natural ingredients, she specializes in allergy-free creations (Margarita herself has a peanut allergy so he understands the pain), and cakes for any occasion and budget using high end ingredients. Mis Pasteles is a minority-owned business by a certified woman and is looking to expand her community and business.
